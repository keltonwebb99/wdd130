Ride into Adventure

Join Dry Oar Boating for an unforgettable journey down the most thrilling rivers. Embrace the spray, live the adventure, and create memories that last a lifetime. Dive in and call our number - your raft awaits!

Main Fork of Salmon River, Idaho

Experience the untamed beauty of Idaho's landscapes on the Salmon River. It offers a blend of calm currents and exciting Class III rapids, perfect for families and thrill-seekers alike. Surround yourself with pine-forested canyons and observe the rich wildlife along the banks as you navigate these pristine waters.

Grand Canyon, Arizona

Dive into the heart of the Grand Canyon with a rafting adventure that will take your breath away. Immerse yourself in the grandeur of one of the world's most iconic natural wonders. With rapids ranging from Class I to V, the river provides an exhilarating ride complemented by the serene beauty of towering canyon walls and ancient geological history.
